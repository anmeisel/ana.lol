“JavaScriptifying” reproductive tasks performs two effects - exposing the technological and artificial fabrication of gender, and labeling the activity as work so that it’s not categorized as a natural part of a person. The meaning of our presumably ambiguous Cyborg switches to “I’d rather be JavaScript than a Goddess”, which acts as a testimony to how programming can help liberate us from essentialist social standards.
